Madrid, Spain

Essential Madrid Activities

Eat
Eat at world renown restaurants in some of the most beautiful scenery you can imagine. Dishes from all over the Mediterranean, including Spain's famous paella are the delightful things you will be feasting on in Madrid.

Visit
Visit historical spots entangled with the lively, modern city. Around every corner is a location, statue, or plaza of historical significance! Or perhaps you will choose to make the short journey out of the city to the beautiful Spanish countryside.

Stay
Stay in the clasiest of hotels in the center of this gem of a city. Be able to walk out of your hotel straight into the bustling streets of La Plaza Mayor or el Puerto Del Sol.

When you get to Madrid, be on the lookout for the cities iconic symbol, a bear rearing up on its hind legs feasting
on berries from the madroño tree!
