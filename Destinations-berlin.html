Berlin,Germany

Why Berlin?

Culture
The food, music, and beer in Berlin are reasons to visit on their own. Berlin has concerts year round for enjoyers of all types of music. After the concert, grab a bite to eat and a locally brewed beer at one of the 14 Michelin-starred restaurants.

History
From the Soviet Memorial in Treptower Park and the remains of the Berlin Wall at East Side Gallery to the chilling Book Burning Square, Berlin has been through a lot and its tumultuous history sets it apart. The c ity’s monuments and museums pay tribute to its tragedies and will give visitors goosebumps and an understanding of the history that continues to shape the city today.

Art
Berlin is known for a plethora of visual art and musical masterminds past and present. Wether you seek a classical piece of Beethoven paired with a classic oil painting, or a grungier piece of political satire street art, Berlin has it all.
