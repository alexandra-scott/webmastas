Cozumel, Mexico

Essential Things to do in Cozumel

Wander the historic streets of San Miguel de Cozumel and visit some of the local shops. Anything from jewelry, to tableware, to clothing; everything is handmade by the locals. You will not find more authentic shopping anywhere.


After you go shopping you must visit the famously natural beaches of the island. There is no place quite like a beautiful natural beach with no other person in sight.
